Instructions:

    1. The competition is of 28 minutes.

    2. The question put in front of you has only ONE INTEGER answer.

    3. You will only proceed if you attempt the question.

    4. You will have 2 attempts to answer the question.

    5. The attempts will carry marks given in the marking scheme i.e. the first attempt will have a marking scheme of +4,0 and the second attempt has marking scheme of +2,-1.

    6. You can log out of the competition any time you want through the LOGOUT button.

    7. You will be given 3 LIFELINES which are explained below.

LIFELINE 1: EXTRA TIME

At the end of 28 minutes if your score is greater than or equal to 12 you can buy time using your score as a currency. There will be no change in the marking scheme. But you won't be able to use the other two lifelines in this extra time.

LIFELINE 2: HOT AND COLD

The lifeline can only be used when your Attempt 1 is incorrect. This lifeline will tell you if Attempt 1 was close to the correct answer or not (± 5%). If Attempt 1 is close to the correct answer then the verdict will be HOT else it will be COLD. The marking scheme for the particular question will be changed to +8 -8.

LIFELINE 3: VISION

The lifeline can only be used when your Attempt 1 is incorrect. This lifeline will tell you the number of digits in Attempt 1 that corresponds with the actual correct answer. The marking scheme for the particular question will be changed to +8 -8.

Hints
1) PERFECT NUMBER is a positive integer that is equal to the sum of its proper divisors.The smallest perfect number is 6,which is the sum of 1,2 and 3.

2) A number n is called deficient if the sum of its proper divisors is less than n and it is called abundant if this sum exceeds n.

3) FIBONACCI NUMBERS is a series of numbers in which each number (Fibonacci Number) is the sum of the two preceding numbers.The Simplest Form is the series 0 , 1 , 1 , 2 , 3 , 5 , 8 , ....

4) PASCAL'S Triangle is a triangle which is denoted as follows:
1
1 1
1 2 1
1 3 3 1

5) A PALINDROMIC NUMBER or NUMERAL PALINDROME is a number that remains the same when its digits are reverse.Like 16461, for example, it is "symmetrical".

6) AMICABLE NUMBER:A pair of numbers,each of which is the sum of the factors of the other. (e.g. 220 & 284.)

7)Armstrong number:A n number n is called armstrong number if the sum of the cube of its individual digits is equal to the number itself.eg:153 :1^3+5^3+3^3=153

8)A Harshad number is a number that is divisible by the sum of its own digits.For example, 1729 is a Harshad number because 1 + 7 + 2 + 9 = 19 and 1729 = 19 × 91

9)A number is called curious if it is equal to the sum of factorials of its digits.For example,145 is a curious number, as 1! + 4! + 5! = 1 + 24 + 120 = 145.
