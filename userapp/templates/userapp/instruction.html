INSTRUCTIONS

    1. The competition is of 28 minutes.

    2. The question put in front of you has only ONE INTEGER answer.

    3. You will only proceed if you attempt the question.

    4. You will have 2 attempts to answer the question.

    5. The attempts will carry marks given in the marking scheme i.e. the first attempt will have a marking scheme of +4,0 and the second attempt has marking scheme of +2,-1.

    6. You can log out of the competition any time you want through the LOGOUT button.

    7. You will be given 3 LIFELINES which are explained below.

LIFELINE 1: EXTRA TIME

At the end of 28 minutes if your score is greater than or equal to 12 you can buy time using your score as a currency. There will be no change in the marking scheme. But you won't be able to use the other two lifelines in this extra time.

LIFELINE 2: HOT AND COLD

The lifeline can only be used when your Attempt 1 is incorrect. This lifeline will tell you if Attempt 1 was close to the correct answer or not (Â± 5%). If Attempt 1 is close to the correct answer then the verdict will be HOT else it will be COLD. The marking scheme for the particular question will be changed to +8 -8.

LIFELINE 3: VISION

The lifeline can only be used when your Attempt 1 is incorrect. This lifeline will tell you the number of digits in Attempt 1 that corresponds with the actual correct answer. The marking scheme for the particular question will be changed to +8 -8.
